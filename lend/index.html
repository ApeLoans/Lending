Lend on Ape.Loans

Earn interest by lending your $APE with customizable terms.

How to Lend

1. Connect your wallet on ApeChain

2. Set your lending terms (interest rate, duration).

3. Match with borrowers and start earning.

Information for Lenders

Benefits: Earn competitive interest rates on your $APE. Your funds are secured by NFT or token collateral, reducing risk.

Terms: Choose your loan duration, and set your interest rate. Loans are automatically matched with borrowers based on your terms.

Risks: While collateral protects your funds, there’s a risk of NFT value fluctuation. In case of default, collateral can be set to liquidate, and you receive 50%-75% of the liquidated profits in $LOAN.

Fees: No fees for lenders. Borrowers pay a 2.5% $APE fee plus Oracle data costs, ensuring you receive your full interest.
