Lend on Ape.Loans

Earn interest by lending your $APE or $LOAN with customizable terms.

How to Lend

1. Connect your wallet to deposit $APE or $LOAN.

2. Set your lending terms (interest rate, duration).

3. Match with borrowers and start earning.

Information for Lenders

Benefits: Earn competitive interest rates on your $APE or $LOAN. Your funds are secured by NFT or token collateral, reducing risk.

Terms: Choose your loan duration and set your interest rate within the platform’s range. Loans are automatically matched with borrowers based on your terms.

Risks: While collateral protects your funds, there’s a risk of NFT value fluctuation. In case of default, collateral is liquidated, and you receive 50%-75% of the liquidated profits

Fees: No fees for lenders. Borrowers pay a 2.5% $APE fee plus Oracle data costs, ensuring you receive your full interest.
